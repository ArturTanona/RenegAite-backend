The CE marking shall be subject to the general principles set out in Article 30 of Regulation (EC) No 765/2008. 2. For high-risk AI systems provided digitally, a digital CE marking shall be used, only if it can easily be accessed via the interface from which that system is accessed or via an easily accessible machine-readable code or other electronic means. 3. The CE marking shall be affixed visibly, legibly and indelibly for high-risk AI systems.
