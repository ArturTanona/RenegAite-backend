The procedure provided for in Chapter VI of Regulation (EU) 2019/1020 shall apply mutatis mutandis. Article 76 Supervision of testing in real world conditions by market surveillance authorities 1. Market surveillance authorities shall have competences and powers to ensure that testing in real world conditions is in accordance with this Regulation. 2. Where testing in real world conditions is conducted for AI systems that are supervised within an AI regulatory sandbox under Article 58, the market surveillance authorities shall verify the compliance with Article 60 as part of their supervisory role for the AI regulatory sandbox.
