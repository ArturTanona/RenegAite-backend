CHAPTER VII GOVERNANCE SECTION 1 Governance at Union level Article 64 AI Office 1. The Commission shall develop Union expertise and capabilities in the field of AI through the AI Office. 2. Member States shall facilitate the tasks entrusted to the AI Office, as reflected in this Regulation. Article 65 Establishment and structure of the European Artificial Intelligence Board 1. A European Artificial Intelligence Board (the ‘Board’) is hereby established. 2. The Board shall be composed of one representative per Member State.
